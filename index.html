Welcome to ScriptSchedule

Your family prescription manager. Get started in 5 steps.

1
Add your family — tap + Add at the top to add each family member by name.
2
Add medications — tap Meds then + Add Med, or use Scan to photograph a prescription label and auto-fill.
3
Install on your phone — add ScriptSchedule to your home screen like a real app:

🍎 iPHONE (Safari)
1. Tap the Share button ⬆️ at the bottom
2. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen ➕
3. Tap Add in the top right — done!
🤖 ANDROID (Chrome)
1. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top right
2. Tap Add to Home Screen or Install App
3. Tap Install — it works just like a real app!

Sharing with your family

🏠 Right now (beta): Designed for one main parent or caregiver to manage medications for everyone in your household — your kids, your spouse, your aging parents, and your pets — all from one phone.

💡 Coming soon: True family sync — multiple family members logging in from different phones to see and update the same medication list.

📱 For now, if you want your spouse to have access, share your phone with them, or have them install ScriptSchedule on their phone with their own list. Your beta feedback will shape how family sharing works in the next version.
5
About reminders ⏰

⏰ Two layers of reminders:

1. In-app: When the app is open, you'll get a full-screen reminder with sound and vibration at every dose time.

2. Phone notifications: Even when ScriptSchedule is closed, you can get a push notification on your phone. We'll ask permission after you add your first medication. (iPhone users: install to home screen first via Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.)

💡 Coming soon: SMS backup if you miss a dose.
