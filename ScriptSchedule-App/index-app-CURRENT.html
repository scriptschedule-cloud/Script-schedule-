Welcome to ScriptSchedule

Your family prescription manager. Get started in 4 steps.

1
Add your family — tap + Add at the top to add each family member by name.
2
Add medications — tap Meds then + Add Med, or use Scan to photograph a prescription label and auto-fill.
3
Install on your phone — add ScriptSchedule to your home screen like a real app:

🍎 iPHONE (Safari)
1. Tap the Share button ⬆️ at the bottom
2. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen ➕
3. Tap Add in the top right — done!
🤖 ANDROID (Chrome)
1. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top right
2. Tap Add to Home Screen or Install App
3. Tap Install — it works just like a real app!
4
Share with family — anyone in your household can use ScriptSchedule on their own phone:

🔗 Tap the share icon in your browser and send the link to a spouse, caregiver, or adult child.

👤 Each person installs it the same way (see step 3). Each phone keeps its own data — perfect for caregivers tracking a parent's medications independently.
